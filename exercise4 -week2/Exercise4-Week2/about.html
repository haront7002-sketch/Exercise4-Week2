ABOUT US

GLOW and GRACE is a jewellery brand rooted in artisan craftsmanship, where each piece is lovingly hand-made and imbued with a signature blend of elegance and individuality. The designer works with care and patience to craft jewellery using techniques ranging from crocheting fine yarns to combining silver, gold and gemstones—ensuring that every item is unique. The brand embraces the French-meaning of “coquette” — chic, stylish, flirtatious — and translates it into pieces meant to help the wearer “Adorn yourself in Romance” What’s particularly special is that the brand isn’t about mass production, but about slow, thoughtful creation: designs that move from idea to hand to finished piece, each one bearing the imprint of the maker’s attention and passion.
